Sectors We Serve

Specialised facilities management solutions designed for the unique requirements of public sector organisations.

Public Sector

Local Authorities

We partner with local councils and government bodies to deliver comprehensive facilities management services that support public services and community infrastructure. Our solutions help local authorities maintain their property portfolios efficiently while meeting strict regulatory requirements and budget constraints.

  • Civic building maintenance and management
  • Public facility operations and upkeep
  • Compliance and regulatory management
  • Cost-effective service delivery
Residential

Housing Associations

We provide tailored facilities management services to housing associations, ensuring that residential properties are maintained to the highest standards. Our focus is on creating safe, comfortable living environments while maximising the value of your housing stock and enhancing tenant satisfaction.

  • Responsive repairs and maintenance
  • Void property preparation and refurbishment
  • Planned maintenance programmes
  • Tenant liaison and support services
Education

Education Sector

We support schools, colleges, and universities with facilities management services that create optimal learning environments. Our services ensure educational institutions can focus on their core mission while we maintain safe, efficient, and inspiring facilities.

  • School building maintenance
  • Grounds and estate management
  • Health and safety compliance
  • Summer works and projects
Healthcare

Healthcare Facilities

We provide specialised facilities management for healthcare environments, understanding the critical importance of maintaining safe, hygienic, and compliant facilities. Our services support the delivery of quality patient care through excellent estate management.

  • Clinical environment maintenance
  • Infection control compliance
  • Critical systems maintenance
  • 24/7 emergency response
