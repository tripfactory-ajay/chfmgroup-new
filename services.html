Services We Offer

At CHFM Group, we specialise in providing comprehensive facilities management solutions tailored to the specific needs of local authorities and housing associations.

Comprehensive Facilities Management and Maintenance

At CHFM Group, we provide end-to-end facilities management services, ensuring the smooth operation and maintenance of your properties. From routine inspections and preventative maintenance to rapid response for emergency repairs, we handle all aspects of facilities management with precision and care.

Our proactive approach guarantees that your buildings are well-maintained, compliant with regulations, and function efficiently to minimise downtime and extend the life of your assets.

Tailored Solutions for Housing Associations and Local Authorities

We understand the unique needs of housing associations and local authorities, offering bespoke facilities management services to support the day-to-day operations of residential and communal spaces.

Our tailored solutions include tenant support, regular property inspections, and efficient management of maintenance requests. By working closely with you, we ensure that properties remain safe, secure, and comfortable, ultimately enhancing the wellbeing of tenants and residents while maintaining the value of your assets.

Energy Management and Sustainability Solutions

Energy efficiency is a key focus of our services at CHFM Group. We help clients reduce energy consumption, lower operational costs, and meet sustainability targets.

Through detailed energy audits, we identify areas for improvement and implement energy-saving measures, such as the installation of energy-efficient systems and technologies.

Our sustainability initiatives are designed to minimise environmental impact and help you achieve a greener, more cost-effective facility.
